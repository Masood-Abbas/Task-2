Muhammad Tariq is more than just a serial entrepreneur; he’s a storyteller, a mentor, and a philanthropist on a mission. As the host of Diary of a Founder, he and his guests share candid insights and real-world lessons with aspiring entrepreneurs and business leaders. Having built successful 7-figure businesses, Tariq is now equally focused on helping struggling entrepreneurs through the Diary of a Founder Foundation. His journey is a testament to the blend he maintains between entrepreneurship, personal well-being, and family life—all driven by a deep sense of purpose.
