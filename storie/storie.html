The Story

Early life and education

Muhammad Tariq’s journey is a testament to the power of determination and vision. Born in the small town of Kamalia, Pakistan, Tariq’s early years were fueled by an insatiable curiosity and a deep desire to make a meaningful impact. This drive led him to the UK, where he pursued a law degree and was called to the Bar at the prestigious Lincoln's Inn in London.

Read More >

Legal practice

Returning to Pakistan, Tariq practiced law in the bustling cities of Lahore and Karachi.But it was in 2007, when he moved to Dubai, that his journey truly took a transformative turn. Joining one of the most prominent law firms in the Middle East, Tariq found success in his legal career, but something inside him craved more.

Read More >

Becoming a serial entrepreneur

He realized that his true calling wasn’t just in practicing law—it was in creating.something enduring, something that would empower others to achieve greatness.

This realization marked the beginning of an extraordinary shift. Transitioning from law to entrepreneurship isn’t easy, but Tariq was determined to make it happen. He took a leap of faith and founded his own law firm, Legal Inz, followed by InZone. But these ventures were more than just businesses—they were platforms designed to create success stories. Leveraging his legal expertise and the diverse experiences he had gathered along the way, Tariq built InZone Group with a singular vision: to help others succeed.

Under his leadership, InZone has seen rapid growth, with ambitious plans for further expansion and a future IPO. Yet, for Tariq, it’s not just about the numbers—it’s about the lives he touches and the impact he makes.

Diary of a Founder Foundation

Tariq has established the Diary of a Founder Foundation with a heartfelt purpose: to support struggling entrepreneurs. Through this foundation, he provides education, training, and access to essential services like legal, corporate, taxation, accounting, and mentorship—ensuring that those who need it most have the resources to turn their dreams into reality.
