DOAF Foundation

Tariq has established the Diary of a Founder Foundation

with a heartfelt purpose: to support struggling entrepreneurs. Through this foundation, he provides education, training, and access to essential services like legal, corporate, taxation, accounting, and mentorship—ensuring that those who need it most have the resources to turn their dreams into reality.

The Diary of a Founder Foundation is here to provide the support, education, and resources needed to help turn your dreams into reality.
